Veronica Bramlett

Veronica Bramlett is a puppeteer with an A.A. in Theatre from Gordon College and B.A. in Theatre and Performance Sudies, focus in Acting, from Kennesaw State University. She has been building and preforming puppets since 2012.

Veronica Bramlett first got into preforming at a young age in ballett, but really got the Theatre bug when she was 11, cast as one of the many children in The King and I at Clayton Alliance for Summer Theater, or CAST. From the ages of 13-19 she spent her summers working behind the scenes or preforming in the various productions each season.
